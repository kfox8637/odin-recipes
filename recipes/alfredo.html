Alfredo Gnocchi Bake

Rich and Creamy Alfredo Gnocchi Bake: A Heavenly Comfort Dish

Alfredo Gnocchi Bake is an indulgent and satisfying dish that combines soft, pillowy gnocchi with a rich, velvety Alfredo sauce, creating a comforting meal perfect for any occasion. The gnocchi, gently baked to a golden finish, absorbs the creamy sauce, infusing every bite with its decadent flavor. The sauce itself is made with butter, heavy cream, garlic, and Parmesan cheese, simmered together to create a luscious, smooth texture. The dish is often topped with a layer of melted mozzarella and Parmesan, which crisps up beautifully in the oven, adding both flavor and a delightful texture contrast.

This dish offers the perfect blend of savory and creamy goodness, with the gnocchi providing a hearty base that complements the rich Alfredo sauce. As it bakes, the flavors deepen and meld together, resulting in a truly comforting experience. Whether served as a standalone meal or alongside a fresh salad, Alfredo Gnocchi Bake is sure to become a favorite go-to dish for those who enjoy indulgent, flavor-packed comfort food. Its creamy texture and hearty gnocchi make every bite feel like a warm, satisfying embrace.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly shaved Parmesan cheese
  • 18 ounces refrigerated gnocchi
  • 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
